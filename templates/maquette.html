Cyber Deaf

Why Cyber Deaf?

Cyber Deaf was created to help bridge the gap between deaf individuals and the world. Our mission is to ensure that everyone, regardless of hearing ability, can communicate, access services, and be part of the online community with ease and dignity.

Our Services

We offer a range of services tailored to support deaf users, including:

  • Text-to-speech conversion
  • Sign language translation through video
  • Community chat features
  • Support for medical communication
